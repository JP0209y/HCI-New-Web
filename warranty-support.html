02

Warranty Details

HCI offers onsite warranty coverage tailored to the purchase price of the massage chair:

In the price segment of below ₹1,50,000, get a 1-Year Onsite Warranty, wherein our expert professionals will visit your location to diagnose and replace the faulty parts at zero cost within the warranty period.

Above ₹1,50,000: Rejoice in an all-inclusive 3-Year Onsite Warranty. In the unlikely event of any trouble, our qualified service team will promptly fix the issue onsite and replace the spoiled component with a brand-new one, with minimal disruption to your convenience.

Extended Service Support – 7 Years: Aside from the standard onsite warranty, HCI guarantees long-term customer satisfaction with an additional 7 years of service support (chargeable). Within the stated years, our technical support is at your service at all times for support, diagnostics, and maintenance service to keep your massage chair always in perfect shape. Service support involves a typical visit fee for the technician, as well as any appropriate repair or replacement fees based on the kind of issue.

In any support-related concern, kindly reach out to us at +91 7300000739.
