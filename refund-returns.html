02

Damaged or Defective Products

In the rare event that your HCI massage chair or massager arrives damaged or defective, we offer a hassle-free replacement policy. Please follow these steps to initiate the replacement process:

notifications_active

Notification

Contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving your product via email or phone

description

Provide Details

Share your order number, a clear description of the issue, and photos/videos showing the damage or defect

fact_check

Verification

Our team will review the details and confirm the issue

autorenew

Replacement

Once verified, we will send a replacement product at no additional cost. The replacement will be the same model and configuration as your original purchase
